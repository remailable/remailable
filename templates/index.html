ReMailable

Email documents to your ReMarkable tablet.




1. Setup

Write an email to remailable@getneutrality.org, and make the subject line a new 8-character one-time code.

You only have to do this step once ever ever ever!


2. Usage

Send a PDF to remailable@getneutrality.org from that email address, and it will magically* appear on your ReMarkable Tablet. (You will receive an email with a confirmation, or an indication of why your message failed.)

3. That's It!

I would love your insight and code review if you are concerned about user privacy.

The source code for this project is available on GitHub, and you can contact me on Twitter at @j6m8.



This project is NOT affiliated with ReMarkable.

At any time, you can send an email with the subject line "Unsubscribe" (case-insensitive) to delete your account and prevent all further emails.
